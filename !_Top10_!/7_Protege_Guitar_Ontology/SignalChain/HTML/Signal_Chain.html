Class: Signal Chain

Documentation: Class to represent the definition of a signal chain through the "contains-a" and "is-part-of-a" inverse relations. The overall focus of this ontology is to show the different pieces of musical equipment that famous artists include in their signal chains, so the purpose of this class is to define the term signal chain itself. The "amplifier", "cable", "effects_pedals", and "guitar" classes are used for sample instances of signal chains to further illustrate this idea. Five sample signal chain instances are represented in this program. Amplifiers, guitars, and cabling are all required to make a signal chain. Signal chains can be constructed without effects pedals, but most include them.
