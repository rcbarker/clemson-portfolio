Abstract Class: Electric Guitarist

Documentation: Top level class in heirachy for all of the electric guitarists represented. It is divided into subclasses by the decade each guitarist started playing. It starts at 1950 and goes by decade until 2000, at which point the guitarist is considered a "modern guitarist". Slots include the name of the guitarist, the year the guitarist began, and a "uses" slot to store every instance of equipment that the particular guitarist uses. Note that "uses" is the inverse slot of the audio equipment "used by" slot. These slots facilitate one of the primary purpose of the ontology (Linking famous artists to the musical equipment they use).
