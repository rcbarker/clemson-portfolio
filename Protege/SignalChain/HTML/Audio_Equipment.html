Abstract Class: Audio Equipment

Documentation: Top level class in heirachy for all of the music equipment represented. Includes electric guitars, amplifiers, and effects pedals. Slots at this level include one for the make and model of the equipment, one for model-specific features of the equipment, and a "used by" slot for relation with instances of the electric guitarists that use each piece of equipment. Note that "used by" is the inverse slot of the electric guitarist "uses" slot. These slots facilitate one of the primary purposes of the ontology (Linking famous artists to the musical equipment they use).
