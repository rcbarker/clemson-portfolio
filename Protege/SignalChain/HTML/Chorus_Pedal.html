Class: Chorus Pedal

Documentation: Subclass for all represented chorus pedals. A chorus pedal is a loop level pedal that manipulates the pitch of a guitar to make it sound like multiple instruments. The primary control of a chorus pedal manipulates the pitch of the modified signal.
