Class: Digital Amplifier

Documentation: Class for all amplifiers utilizing digital amplification. Digital amplifiers typically have built in effects similiar to effects pedals, so the default value for the "on board effects" slot is true. They also have an extra slot for thier mounting type, which is either cabinet or rack based.
