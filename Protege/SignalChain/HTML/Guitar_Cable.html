Class: Guitar Cable

Documentation: Class to represent any form of guitar cables. Cables are necessary for constructing a signal chain, but science has not yet shown tonal difference between two quality, shiedled cables. For illustration, I will represent two instances of cables, but cables are not the focus of this ontology.
